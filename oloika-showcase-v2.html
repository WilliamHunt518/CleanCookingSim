Scalability

Capacity first

Model before you plug in: Oloika's upgrade (25 kWp / 54 kWh) left ~50% daytime headroom.

2

Finance locally

Sacco-backed sales: subsidies 40% → 25% → 0% within a year; MSSL now invests its own capital.

3

Signal, don't lecture

Green LED + 25% discount → 57% daylight cooking, rising monthly.

4

Rehearse, then price

Re-run the simulator per site: new menu, personas, PV curve — same score: wood share + 5 × P(cap breach).

Revenue: ~KES 2,100/mo now → KES 4,200/mo at 4 kWh/day · wood_share is the number carbon credits (Gold Standard / Verra) are written against.
